Welcome to Job Application Tracker

Job application tracker (JAT) as the name describes allows you to prioritize and track the job applications and interests from various job searching platforms such as LinkedIn, Stepstone, Indeed and so on. You can simply add the jobs you are interested in and assign it to different roles, status and priority. Once applied visit JAT again and change the status. Additionally, while applying for the job, you can go through the checklist to make sure you have a strong job application!!
